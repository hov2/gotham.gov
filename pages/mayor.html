Gotham City is governed by Mayor Hamilton Hill and the City Council with the assistance of various departments, agencies, and commissions.
MEET THE MAYOR

Mayor Hamilton Hill is an accomplished advocate for working people and a proud product of Gotham City. He supported the building of Stonegate Penitentiary with sponsorship from the Wayne Foundation, as well as the dedication of a new courthouse and improved public transport.
